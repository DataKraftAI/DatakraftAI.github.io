Data→Insights Copilot

Upload a CSV → trends, simple charts, and 3–5 prioritized business actions.

Problem
Leaders want quick, trustworthy takeaways from messy data without hiring an analyst.
My Solution
Streamlit app that profiles the CSV, charts time & categories, and (optionally) uses GPT to propose actions tailored to Exec/Ops/Marketing.
How It Works
  • CSV upload → pandas profile
  • Matplotlib charts (time series & top categories)
  • Optional AI call (OpenAI) with a Chain-of-Thought prompt to produce findings & actions
