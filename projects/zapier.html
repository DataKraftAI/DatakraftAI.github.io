Zapier AI Workflow

Form → AI → email delivery. A packaged automation with access control.

Problem
Teams want automated, consistent outputs from simple inputs (e.g., weekly posts or status reports).
My Solution
Tally form → Zapier → OpenAI → Gmail. Includes an allowlist check and friendly quota message if exceeded.
How It Works
  • Client fills Tally form (fields → variables)
  • Zapier assembles a prompt & calls OpenAI
  • Results are emailed to the client, with simple HTML formatting
Deliverables
Prompt templates, Zapier blueprint, error/limit handling copy, and screenshots.
