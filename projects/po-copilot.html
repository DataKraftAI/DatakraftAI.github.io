For Product Owners

Product Owner’s Copilot

AI assistant for backlog refinement & acceptance criteria

Problem

Backlog items often arrive as rough notes or long paragraphs. Converting them into clear, testable user stories with acceptance criteria takes time and varies by writer and team.

My Solution

A lightweight copilot that takes raw ideas or epic notes and produces ready-to-groom stories:

  • Rewrites into INVEST-style user stories
  • Generates Gherkin-style acceptance criteria (Given/When/Then)
  • Suggests priority (High/Medium/Low) and complexity hints
  • Flags duplicates or unclear scope

How It Works

  • PO pastes notes, epic description, or raw bullet points
  • Copilot normalizes language and applies story templates
  • Produces acceptance criteria and edge cases
  • Outputs a tidy list ready for import to Jira/Linear

Deliverables

  • INVEST-compliant stories (“As a … I want … so that …”)
  • Acceptance criteria in Gherkin format
  • Priority suggestion + assumptions and open questions
  • CSV/Markdown export for backlog tools

Tech Stack

Python, Streamlit, OpenAI API, Pandas · Optional: export helpers for Jira/Linear
