Meeting & Email Minutes

Paste raw text → get clean minutes, owners, deadlines, and follow-ups.

Problem
Managers waste time turning messy notes into clear action lists.
My Solution
A paste-in summarizer that outputs sections: Decisions, Action Items (with owners/dates), Risks, and Open Questions.
How It Works
  • Paste meeting transcript or email thread
  • LLM applies a structured template with sections
  • Optional: export to Markdown or copy to clipboard
